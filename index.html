The Kinetics and Chemical Technology Laboratory
Wolfson Department of Chemical Engineering · Technion — IIT

Autonomous platforms that predict chemical reactions before the lab does.

We combine chemical engineering, software engineering, and quantum chemistry theory to build open-source AI-driven tools to predict how real-world reactive systems behave. We accelerate chemical R&D by autonomous wet-lab validation. Our focus is on sustainable fuels, energy, polymers, pharma, and biochemistry.

Research directions

A full-stack approach, from algorithms to self-driving labs.

Spanning core algorithms, resilient materials and sustainable fuels, and self-driving laboratories that close the loop between computation and experiment.

01

Theory

Accurate thermodynamics of complex species, automated transition-state searches with neural networks and generative methods, and quantum-driven refinement of chemical kinetic models.

02

Engineering

Generative search of novel molecules against multi-variable targets, and cyber-physical systems for automated hypothesis generation, refinement, validation, and revision.

03

Applications

Sustainable aviation fuels, satellite propellants, and CO₂ conversion in energy; drug shelf-life and retro-synthesis in pharma; 2D materials and block co-polymers in electronics.

Accessibility statement

Scope. This declaration covers the entire Dana Research Group website — this home page, the publications page, and the site map. The site aims to conform to the Israeli Standard IS 5568 (based on WCAG 2.0 level AA) and the Equal Rights for Persons with Disabilities (Accessibility Adjustments to a Service) Regulations, 2013. It provides semantic structure and landmarks, full keyboard navigation with a visible focus indicator, a skip-to-content link, an accessibility toolbar (text size, contrast, and display adjustments), and text alternatives for images, and it honours your system settings for reduced motion, contrast, and text zoom.

Known limitations. As a research-group site, some content — in particular older publication PDFs — may not be fully accessible. If you find any content you are unable to use, please contact the accessibility coordinator below and we will make it accessible to you, ordinarily within 60 days of your request.

The Fundamental and Applied Chemical Kinetics Lab is located in the Wolfson Department of Chemical Engineering, Room 313, Technion City, Haifa; the facility is accessible via elevators. The group does not routinely receive the public on-site; visits are by prior arrangement, and accessibility accommodations can be arranged in advance via the coordinator.

Reporting an accessibility problem. If you encounter an accessibility barrier on this site, please contact our accessibility coordinator, Assoc. Prof. Alon Grinberg Dana (Group head): dana.group@technion.ac.il, +972-73-378-2117. Please describe the problem, the page, and what you were trying to do. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Commission for Equal Rights of Persons with Disabilities at the Ministry of Justice, which oversees enforcement of the accessibility regulations.
